About Me

I am a dedicated mathematics researcher with a strong foundation in abstract algebra and number theory, currently specializing in Algebraic Geometry. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's in Mathematics from COMSATS University Islamabad.

During my undergraduate studies, I independently pursued advanced topics in Algebraic Geometry under the guidance of my supervisor, exploring the fundamental connections between geometric intuition and algebraic formalism. This interdisciplinary approach has allowed me to apply geometric principles to computational challenges.

My research focuses on understanding the geometric nature of algebraic varieties and schemes, with particular interest in their computational aspects and applications to modern mathematical problems.
