Events Organized

International Conference on Applied and Computational Mathematics

Organizer & Technical Coordinator
2025

I had the privilege of serving as the Organizer and Technical Coordinator for the International Conference on Applied and Computational Mathematics (ICACM 2025). This prestigious event brought together leading mathematicians, researchers, and academics from around the world to discuss cutting-edge developments in mathematical sciences.

My responsibilities included managing all technical aspects of the conference, coordinating with international speakers, overseeing audio-visual setups, and ensuring seamless execution of parallel sessions. The conference featured 15+ international speakers and attracted over 200 participants, creating a vibrant platform for knowledge exchange and collaboration.

Q K Ghori Alumni Seminar Series

Organizer & Technical Coordinator
2024-2025

I organized and coordinated the first five sessions of the Q K Ghori Alumni Seminar Series, creating a platform for knowledge sharing between alumni and current students. This initiative brought together successful graduates who shared their academic and professional journeys, providing valuable insights and mentorship opportunities.

Each session focused on different aspects of mathematical research and career development, featuring alumni working in diverse fields from academic research to industry applications. My role involved end-to-end coordination including speaker invitations, technical setup, audience management, and post-event follow-ups.

Tri-Math Quiz Competition

Event Organizer
2024

I organized the Tri-Math Quiz Competition, an inter-departmental mathematics competition that challenged students' problem-solving abilities and mathematical knowledge. The event brought together 15 teams from different departments, creating a vibrant atmosphere of academic competition and collaboration.

The competition featured multiple rounds including speed mathematics, problem-solving challenges, and theoretical questions. My responsibilities included designing the competition structure, coordinating with faculty judges, managing team registrations, and overseeing the entire event execution. The event was well-received and helped foster greater interest in mathematical problem-solving among students.

Venue
COMSATS University Islamabad
Date
November 2024
Participants
15 Teams, 45 Students
Competition
3 Rounds, Multiple Categories

One-Day Symposium on Emerging Trends in Mathematics, AI, and Computing

Organizer & Technical Coordinator
2025

The SIAM Student Chapter at COMSATS University Islamabad organized a One-Day Symposium on Emerging Trends in Mathematics, AI, and Computing in collaboration with Middle Tennessee State University, USA. This event brought together students, researchers, and academicians to explore the foundational role of mathematics in advancing artificial intelligence and modern computing.

My primary responsibilities included managing all technical aspects of the symposium and overseeing event operations to ensure smooth execution. Distinguished speakers discussed neural networks, data from pebbles to quantum bytes, and the mathematical principles driving today's AI innovations.

Venue
EE Seminar Hall, COMSATS University Islamabad
Date
13th November, 2025
Participants
Students, Researchers & Academicians
Collaboration

Teaching Experience

Workshop on LaTeX and Python for Beginners - 1

Lead Instructor & Organizer
2024

This comprehensive workshop introduced students to the fundamentals of LaTeX typesetting and Python programming. The beginner-level course was designed to provide a solid foundation in both technologies, focusing on practical applications for academic and research purposes.

Participants learned LaTeX document preparation, mathematical typesetting, and basic Python programming concepts. The workshop emphasized hands-on learning with real-world examples and exercises that helped students immediately apply their new skills to their academic work.

Venue
COMSATS University Islamabad
Date
March 2024
Participants
50+ Students
Duration
2 Days Intensive Workshop

Workshop on LaTeX and Python for Beginners - 2

Lead Instructor & Organizer
2025

Building on the success of the first workshop, this second iteration expanded the curriculum to include advanced topics and more complex projects. Due to high demand and positive feedback, we were re-invited to conduct this follow-up workshop with an enhanced curriculum.

The workshop covered advanced LaTeX features including bibliography management, custom commands, and presentation design, alongside intermediate Python programming concepts including data analysis libraries and automation scripts. Participants completed comprehensive projects that demonstrated their proficiency in both technologies.
